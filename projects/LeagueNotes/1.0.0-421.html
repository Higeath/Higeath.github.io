CHAMPION BALANCE CHANGES

Kennen

Passive - Mark of the Storm

Kennen's abilities add Marks of the Storm to opponents they hit, which explode at 3 stacks, stunning the opponent and restoring some of Kennen's energy.

NEW
Q - Thundering Shuriken

Kennen throws a lightning infused shuriken towards a location, damaging the first enemy it hits and adding a Mark of the Storm to them.

NEW
W - Electrical Surge

Kennen passively deals extra damage and adds a Mark of the Storm to opponents every few attacks, and can activate this ability to deal damage to all nearby marked units and add another mark.

NEW
E - Lightning Rush

Kennen transforms into a writhing ball of electricity, receiving a massive energy boost and gaining the ability to move through units. Enemy units he passes through receive damage and gain a Mark of the Storm.

NEW
R - Slicing Maelstrom

Kennen summons an electrical storm around him, casting down lightning bolts to a random enemy champions near him multiple times per second.

NEW
