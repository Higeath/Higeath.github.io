CHAMPION BALANCE CHANGES

NEWQuinn

Passive - Harrier

Valor periodically marks enemies as Vulnerable. Quinn's first attack against Vulnerable targets will deal bonus physical damage.

NEW
Q - Blinding Assault

Quinn sends Valor forth in a straight line skillshot. Valor stops at the first enemy struck, damaging and briefly blinding all nearby enemies.

NEW

Tag Team: Valor damages and blinds all nearby enemies.

NEW
W - Heightened Senses

(Passive): Attacking a Vulnerable target will increase Quinn's attack speed for a few seconds.

NEW

(Active): Valor rushes to the skies, revealing a large area around Quinn, including all brush and non-stealthed units.

NEW

Tag Team (Passive): Valor gains a large amount of attack speed permanently.

NEW

Tag Team (Active): Valor reveals a large area around him, including brush and non-stealthed units.

NEW
E - Vault

Quinn dashes to an enemy, dealing damage and slowing them briefly. She then kicks back off her target, landing near her maximum attack range.

NEW

Tag Team: Valor dashes to an enemy, damaging and slowing them for a brief time.

NEW
R - Tag Team

Quinn leaps away from the battlefield and allows Valor to take her place briefly. Valor functions as a mobile melee attacker and gains a huge movement speed bonus when out of combat. This bonus is decreased gradually to a smaller value when in combat.

NEW

Skystrike: Valor flies from the battlefield, allowing Quinn to return in a hail of bolts that damage all nearby enemies. Damage is increased based on how much health targets are missing. If Tag Team is not cast a second time, Skystrike is cast automatically once Tag Team ends.
