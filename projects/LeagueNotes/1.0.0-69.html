CHAMPION BALANCE CHANGES

Poppy

Passive - Valiant Fighter

Any damage dealt to Poppy that exceeds 10% of her current health is reduced by 50%. This doesn’t affect damage dealt by structures.

NEW
Q - Devastating Blow

Poppy crushes her opponent, dealing base damage plus a flat amount and a percentage of her target’s max health as a bonus damage. The bonus damage cannot exceed a threshold based on rank.

NEW
W - Paragon of Demacia

Passive: Upon being hit or attacking an enemy, Poppy's armor and damage are increased by a flat amount for 5 seconds. This effect can stack 10 times.

NEW

Active: Poppy gains max stacks of Paragon of Demacia and her Movement Speed is increased for 5 seconds.

NEW
E - Heroic Charge

Poppy charges at an enemy and carries them for a short distance. The initial impact deals a small amount of damage, and if they collide with terrain, her target will take a high amount of damage and be stunned.

NEW
R - Diplomatic Immunity

Poppy focuses intently on a single target dealing increased damage to them. Poppy is immune to any damage and abilities from enemies other than her target.

NEW
