CHAMPION BALANCE CHANGES

Sona

Passive - Power Chord

After casting 3 spells, Sona's next attack deals bonus magic damage. Additionally, Sona's Auras persist for 3 seconds after deactivating and set off a 2 second global cooldown.

BUFF
Q - Hymn of Valor

Sona plays the Hymn of Valor, granting nearby allied champions bonus Damage and Ability Power. Additionally, casting this ability sends out bolts of sound, dealing magic damage to the nearest two enemy champions or monsters.

BUFF
W - Aria of Perseverance

Sona plays the Aria of Perseverance, granting nearby allied champions bonus Armor and Magic Resist. Additionally, casting this ability sends out healing melodies, healing Sona and a nearby wounded ally.

BUFF
E - Song of Celerity

Sona plays the Song of Celerity, granting nearby allied champions bonus Movement Speed. Additionally, casting this ability energizes nearby allies with a burst of speed.

BUFF
R - Crescendo

Sona plays her ultimate chord, forcing enemy champions to dance, taking magic damage over time.

BUFF

Blitzcrank

E - Power Fist

Now resets the auto-attack timer.

NEW

Fiddlesticks

Q - Terrify

Will now grant assists.

NEW

Garen

Passive - Perseverance

Is no longer removed by combat dehancing effects such as Blood Scent.

CHANGE
W - Courage

Damage reduction reduced to 30% from 35%.

NERF
R - Demacian Justice

Fixed various bugs that caused Demacian Justice to not work correctly sometimes.

BUGFIX

Gragas

R - Explosive Cask

Now interacts properly with spell shields like Banshee's Veil.

BUGFIX

Jax

E - Counter Strike

Now has a buff showing the duration of the effect.

NEW

Karthus

Passive - Death Defied

It now also grants 20% death timer reduction to compensate for the 8 seconds Karthus is active after dying.

NEW

The code has been remade to fix some bugs and usability issues.

NEW

Kennen

E - Lightning Rush

Is no longer castable while disabled.

CHANGE

Kassadin

W - Nether Blade

Mana cost reduced to 25 at all ranks from 30/40/50/60/70.

BUFF
R - Riftwalk

It no longer shows cast particles over fog of war.

CHANGE

It now leaves a buff showing the duration of the increased cost and damage of subsequent casts.

CHANGE

Katarina

Passive - Voracity

No longer reduces true damage when used with Shunpo.

CHANGE
E - Shunpo

No longer ignores enemy spell shields like Banshee's Veil.

CHANGE

Kog'Maw

E - Caustic Spittle

Fixed a bug where Caustic Spittle was giving multiplicative attack speed rather than base.

BUGFIX

Rammus

W - Defensive Ball Curl

Can now be cancelled.

NEW

Shaco

W - Jack In The Box

Fixed a bug where fearing enemies didn't grant an assist.

BUGFIX

Singed

NEW

Taric

R - Radiance

NEW

Twitch

Q - Ambush

The first attack after breaking stealth will now benefit from the bonus attack speed.

CHANGE

Vladimir

CHANGE
