ITEM BALANCE CHANGES

General

Changed all aura-granting items so that clones will not grant a double bonus to the aura holder. Clones with aura items otherwise interact the same with other champions.

CHANGE

Guardian Angel and Banshee's Veil will now only proc on cloned units if it was ready to activate when the clone was created. In other words, clones now sync their Guardian Angel and Banshee's Veil states to the main champion when created.

CHANGE
Archangel's Staff

Mana to ability power ratio increased to 3% from 2.5%.

BUFF
Innervating Locket

Effects now only give half benefit to allies.

NERF

No longer heals minions.

CHANGE
Will of the Ancients

will now grant double bonus to the holders if multiple members of a team have the item, like all other aura items.

CHANGE
Spirit Visage

Healing and regen bonus reduced to 20% from 25%.

NERF
Sword of the Divine

Active duration increased to 8 seconds from 5.

BUFF
Mercury's Treads

Crowd control reduction reduced to 35% from 40%.

NERF
Guardian Angel

Clones that expire while being revived with Guardian Angel will no longer last permanently.
