CHAMPION BALANCE CHANGES

Shen

Passive - Ki Strike

Every 8 seconds, Shen's next attack deals bonus damage based on his max health. Being attacked by enemy champions makes this passive occur more frequently.

NEW
Q - Vorpal Blade

Hits a target enemy for magic damage, and marks the target for allies to see. Allied units that attack the marked enemy target get healed over several seconds.

NEW
W - Feint

Shen defends against the next champion attack or spell, blocking much of the damage from it.

NEW
E - Shadow Dash

Shen dashes towards target location, taunting enemies he passes through.

NEW
R - Stand United

Shen places a shield on himself and on a target ally which absorbs damage. After channeling for several seconds, Shen teleports to the target ally.
