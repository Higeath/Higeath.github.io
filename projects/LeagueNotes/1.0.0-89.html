Syndra

Passive - Transcendent

NEW

NEW

Force of Will: Increases slowing duration.

NEW

Scatter the Weak: Spell width increased.

NEW
Q - Dark Sphere

Syndra conjures a Dark Sphere, dealing magic damage. The sphere remains and can be manipulated by her other abilities.

Syndra picks up and throws a minion or Dark Sphere, dealing magic damage and slowing the movement speed of enemies.

NEW

Syndra knocks enemies and Dark Spheres back, dealing magic damage. Enemies hit by Dark Spheres become stunned.

Syndra bombards an enemy champion with all of her Dark Spheres.
