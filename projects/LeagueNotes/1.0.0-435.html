CHAMPION BALANCE CHANGES

Nidalee

Passive - Prowl

Nidalee gains additional movement speed when moving through or out of brush.

NEW
Q - Javelin Toss

Nidalee tosses her javelin at long range, dealing increased damage the farther the javelin travels (up to 250%).

NEW
Q - Takedown

Nidalee jumps at the throat of her target, dealing her attack damage plus a static bonus. The damage of this skill increases the lower life percentage the target has.

NEW
W - Bushwhack

Nidalee creates a camouflaged trap that, when sprung, will reveal the target and deal damage. Champion Armor and Magic Resistance are reduced by a percentage for the duration of the debuff. Traps are visible to enemies.

NEW
W - Pounce

Nidalee pounces forward, dealing damage in a small radius.

NEW
E - Primal Surge

Nidalee calls upon her bestial heritage to heal an ally champion and grant them an attack speed bonus for a short duration.

NEW
E - Swipe

Nidalee slashes wildly in front of her, dealing moderate damage in a large area.

NEW
R - Aspect Of The Cougar

Nidalee may freely transform between a human and a cougar at no cost. While in cougar form all of her attacks are melee and she gains additional dodge and movement speed. Leveling this skill increases the effects of her cougar abilities.
