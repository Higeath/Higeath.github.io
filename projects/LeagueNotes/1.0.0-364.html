ITEM BALANCE CHANGES

General

The gold per 10 component of Philosopher's Stone, Heart of Gold, Kage's Lucky Pick, and Avarice Blade is now unique to itself. This means that it won't stack when the bonus comes from the same item, but it will stack when having different items.

BUFF
Boots of Mobility

Will now mark you in combat for receiving or dealing any damage, even if it is absorbed by shields.

CHANGE
Spirit Visage

It now increases regeneration from lifesteal and spell vamp.

CHANGE

Fixed a bug where it was granting too much bonus regeneration.

BUGFIX
Philosopher's Stone

Health regeneration per 5 reduced to 18 from 22.5.

NERF

Mana regeneration per 5 reduced to 8 from 10.

NERF
Eleisa's Miracle

Combine cost increased to 500 from 400.

NERF
Quicksilver Sash
